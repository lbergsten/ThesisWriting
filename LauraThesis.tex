\thesisabstract{The Standard Model vector boson fusion Higgs boson fiducial and inclusive cross-sections are measured in the $H\rightarrow WW^*\rightarrow\ell\nu\ell\nu$ decay channel using 139 fb$^{-1}$ of proton-proton collisions. Collisions from the Large Hadron Collider at $\sqrt{s}=13$ TeV are recorded by the ATLAS detector. The fiducial and inclusive cross-sections are measured to be 2.8$\pm0.5$ (Stat.) $\pm0.3$ (Theor.) $\pm0.2$ (Exp.)~fb and 89$\pm15$ (Stat.) $\pm10$ (Theor.) $\pm5$ (Exp.)~fb. No significant deviations from Standard Model predictions are observed.}
